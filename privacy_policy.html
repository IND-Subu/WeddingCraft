Privacy Policy

WeddingCraft ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Phone number (for authentication using OTP)

Most information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

3. Data Storage

WeddingCraft is designed with an offline-first approach. Most user data is stored locally on your device storage. We do not maintain central servers containing your wedding data.
